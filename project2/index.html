CRIMEA

A Land Between Mountains and Sea

A unique peninsula where majestic mountains meet the warm sea, and the history and culture of different nations intertwine.

CRIMEA IS ABOUT

NATURE

Majestic mountains, green valleys, and unique wild nature reserves.

SEA

Clear beaches, hidden bays, azure water, and golden sunsets.

HISTORY

Ancient cave cities, medieval fortresses, and khan palaces.

CULTURE

Rich national traditions, hospitality, crafts, and famous cuisine.

The Soul of Heritage

The culture, traditional ornaments, and golden hospitality of the Crimean Tatars are the true heart of the peninsula. Every family heritage is a harmony of customs, deep respect for elders, and endless love for the homeland.

Crimea — a place where the heart finds its home.
